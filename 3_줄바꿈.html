An actuary is a business professional who deals withthe measurement and management of risk and uncertainty (BeAnActuary 2011a).
The name of the corresponding field is actuarial science.
These risks can affect both sides of the balance sheet and require asset management, liability management, and valuation skills (BeAnActuary 2011b).

Actuaries provide assessments of financial security systems, with a focus on their complexity, their mathematics, and their mechanisms (Trowbridge 1989, p. 7).
